Alfredo

Description

This is a recipe for homemade Alfredo sauce. My favorite pastas to use are spaghetti and penne, and my favorite proteins to use are shrimp and chicken. However, you can accomapny this sauce with any pasta or protein of your choosing.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 stick of Butter
  • 2 tbsp of Diced Garlic
  • 16oz of Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 2 tbsp of Cream Chease
  • 8oz of grated Parmesan Cheese
  • Ground Pepper
  • 1lb Pasta of choice

Steps

  1. Start your saucepan on low heat heat.
  2. Add the butter and the garlic and stir
  3. Once thoroughly melted, add your cream cheese and stir
  4. As the cream cheese starts to thin, add your heaviy whipping cream and stir
  5. Stir and get your ingredients well mixed
  6. Add Parmesan cheese and stir
  7. Once you have the cheese mixed and melted in well, add ground pepper to taste.

Additional Info

It's important to make sure that you stir the sauce constantly. You want to make sure and avoid boiling in this process as it will affect the consistency of the sauce.

This entire cooking process should take 10-15min, so you will want to have already started your pasta and protein to ensure they are completed around the same time.
