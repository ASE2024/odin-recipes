Fried Rice

Description

This recipe plays right off of the White Rice recipe. You will want to pay spectial attention to the additional info on that recipe and make sure that you add the frozen vegetables mentioned there.

I would also reccomend a carbon steel wok that's well seasoned for this meal. Of course, that's certainly not a requirement, but will just make this dish easier. Any large pan will do the job.

Ingredients

  • Already cooked White Rice
  • About 1/2 cup of Olive Oil
  • Half a diced onion
  • 2-3 eggs
  • chopped scallions
  • Soy sauce
  • sesame oil

Steps

  1. Drizzle some oil in the pan and start on medium heat
  2. Once your oil is heated and lightly smoking add your diced onion
  3. Make sure to stir/toss frequently to keep from burning
  4. While your oninos sizzle, break you eggs into a bowl and beat with fork
  5. Add your beat eggs and stir/toss frequently
  6. Once eggs are mostly cooked, add your cooked white rice
  7. Making sure to stir/toss frequently, add soy sauce to taste/color
  8. If you have any cooked protiens you wanted to add, this would be the time to do so
  9. Once thoroughly mixed drizzle some sesame oil and toss a final time
  10. Remove from flame and serve with some scallions on top to garnish
